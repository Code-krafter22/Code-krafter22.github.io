01 Profile

I turn raw sequencing and clinical data into reproducible pipelines, validated biomarkers, and results built for both the bench and production.

A Bioinformatics Analyst who bridges academic research and industry-ready pipeline development, specializing in NGS and multi-omics analysis across human patient cohorts and mouse models with linked clinical data, spanning scRNA-seq, bulk RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, spatial transcriptomics, and proteomics.

I build reproducible, version-controlled pipelines (Nextflow/nf-core, Docker, Git) on Linux/SLURM HPC, and apply machine learning for biomarker discovery and disease prediction, work grounded in peer-reviewed research and engineered for real-world, production-scale use. My background as a Doctor of Pharmacy and published researcher lets me speak fluently to computational, clinical, and industry audiences alike.
